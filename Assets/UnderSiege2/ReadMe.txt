================Under Siege 2===============
Requires Unity 2019.4.28 or higher.
-=Under Siege 2=-

Documentation: https://ilonion.com/usiegeunity2

This pack contains modular constructor for creating sci-fi cyberpunk environments: station-like, tech fly islands-like etc.
Includes turret->bullets->enemy attack gameplay;
Includes drills robots->ground attack gameplay.

Suitable for creating colorful scenes, custom gameplay scenes, adding to your wip sci-fi project:
Gameplay modes independent from anything.
It means you may drag and drop it to any project.

built-in, Universal, HDRP is supported.
